Papallacta

Papallacta is the high Andes hot springs escape just outside Quito, set between páramo landscapes and misty mountain air. Come for the thermal pools, quiet views, and a reset day that still feels deeply Ecuador. It is one of the easiest ways to combine nature and relaxation in a single trip.

Region: Andes Best for: Hot springs, relaxation, páramo scenery Typical time: Day trip or 1 night

What Papallacta is best for

Papallacta is for slowing down. Think warm pools in cool mountain air, simple walks, and calm views that do most of the work for you. It is a great add-on before or after a more active itinerary.

Thermal hot springs

A relaxing soak with fresh mountain air and quiet surroundings. Perfect after hiking, long drives, or busy city days.

Páramo landscapes

High-altitude scenery, dramatic skies, and a totally different Ecuador mood than the cloud forest.

Easy escape from Quito

One of the best low-effort nature and wellness days near the capital. Works well even with limited time.

Gentle walks and fresh air

Light activity options that pair well with a soak. Great for couples, families, and mixed-energy groups.

Hot springs Relaxation Páramo Wellness Mountain air Easy from Quito

Simple planning flow

For a day trip, go early, soak, take a short walk, then return before dark. For an overnight, keep the schedule light and give yourself time for a long, unhurried soak.
