The Andes

High-altitude landscapes, volcano views, colonial cities, artisan towns, and epic day trips. This hub is being built to help you explore the Andes with curated highlights and practical planning.

What makes the Andes special

Ecuador’s Andes offer dramatic scenery and rich culture packed into manageable travel distances. One day you can explore a historic city, the next you can hike near volcanoes or relax in a mountain town. This guide will help you find experiences that fit your pace, comfort level, and interests.

Signature highlights

  • Volcano landscapes, crater lakes, and panoramic viewpoints
  • Colonial cities, museums, and historic neighborhoods
  • Artisan markets, craft towns, and cultural traditions
  • Outdoor adventures: hikes, hot springs, and scenic drives
Culture Nature Adventure Photography

Best for

  • Travelers who want a mix of culture and scenery
  • Day trips and itinerary-based exploration
  • Hikers and nature lovers
  • Food, history, and local markets

Trip styles we will cover

  • City + countryside combinations
  • Volcano routes and scenic day trips
  • Wellness: hot springs and slow travel
