The Andes

The Andean region is Ecuador’s cultural and geographic backbone. From colonial cities and mountain viewpoints to markets, volcanoes, and highland towns, this is where history, altitude, and daily life intersect.

Region type: Highlands Best for: Culture, cities, landscapes Includes: Quito, Otavalo, Cotopaxi

What defines the Andes

Ecuador’s Andes stretch north to south through the heart of the country. This region combines historic cities, indigenous traditions, dramatic volcanoes, and some of the most scenic drives in South America.

Travelers often begin here due to international flights arriving in Quito, making the Andes a natural starting point for short stays or longer itineraries.
