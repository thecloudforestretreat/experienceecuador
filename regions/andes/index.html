The Andes

The Andean region is Ecuador’s cultural and geographic backbone. Colonial cities, volcanoes, markets, and highland towns make this the most common starting point for travelers.

Region type: Highlands Best for: Culture, cities, landscapes Includes: Quito, Otavalo, Cotopaxi

What defines the Andes

Ecuador’s Andes stretch north to south through the heart of the country. This region blends indigenous culture, historic cities, dramatic landscapes, and some of South America’s most iconic volcano scenery.

Most international travelers arrive through Quito, making the Andes a natural first stop whether you have one day or several weeks.
