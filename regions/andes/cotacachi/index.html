Cotacachi

Cotacachi is a calm highland town with artisan energy and easy access to one of the north Andes highlights: Cuicocha crater lake. Pair it with Otavalo for a classic day loop, or slow down for a relaxed culture plus nature reset.

What Cotacachi Is Best For

Think of Cotacachi as a low stress base for a beautiful crater lake viewpoint day, plus a walkable town for browsing artisan shops, grabbing lunch, and enjoying the pace of the northern highlands.

Signature Highlights

  • Cuicocha crater lake viewpoints and trail options
  • Easy town strolls, cafes, and local artisan shopping
  • Simple pairing with Otavalo market days
  • Highland landscapes and quick photo stops along the drive
Crater Lake Day Trip Culture Easy Nature

Best For

  • Travelers who want a light, scenic Andes day
  • Otavalo pairing for markets plus nature
  • Photo friendly viewpoints with minimal effort
  • Families and mixed groups with different energy levels

Simple Planning Flow

  • Morning: drive north and reach Cotacachi area early
  • Midday: Cuicocha viewpoints and short walk
  • Late lunch: Cotacachi town (easy, walkable)
  • Optional: Otavalo stop if timing works

What To Do and How To Pair It

Cotacachi works best as a simple half day to full day plan. Build your day around Cuicocha, then use town time for food and browsing. If you want more culture, add Otavalo. If you want a calmer day, skip the extra driving and keep it focused.
