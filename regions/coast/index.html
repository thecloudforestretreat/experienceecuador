The Coast

Sun, surf, seafood culture, and relaxed coastal towns. This hub is being built to help you discover where to go, what to do, and how to plan the perfect coastal escape.

What Makes The Coast Special

Ecuador’s Coast blends beach life with vibrant local culture. Some areas are perfect for unplugging and relaxing, while others are built for surfing, nightlife, and food adventures. This guide will help you match the right places to your travel style and timing.

Signature Highlights

  • Beach towns with distinct vibes, from quiet to energetic
  • Surf spots, ocean viewpoints, and coastal sunsets
  • Seafood culture: ceviches, coastal markets, and local favorites
  • Day trips, nature reserves, and coastal excursions
