Galapagos Islands

A world-famous ecosystem where wildlife, ocean, and volcanic landscapes come together. This hub is being built to help you plan with clarity, confidence, and curated recommendations.

What Makes Galapagos Special

The Galapagos Islands are known for extraordinary biodiversity and strict conservation. Experiences here feel different because nature leads the itinerary. The goal is to help you understand what is worth doing, how long to stay, and what kind of trip fits your style.

Signature Highlights

  • Wildlife encounters: sea lions, giant tortoises, marine iguanas, blue-footed boobies
  • Snorkeling and diving in clear waters with reef life and larger species
  • Volcanic landscapes and dramatic coastlines
  • Guided rules and protected areas that preserve the ecosystem

How To Use This Hub

  • Choose your base island first, then build tours around it
  • Plan trip length by what you want to do most: snorkel, wildlife, or volcano hikes
  • Mix guided day tours with downtime so the trip stays relaxed
  • Keep logistics simple: fewer island moves, better experience
