The Amazon

Jungle rivers, wildlife encounters, and immersive eco experiences. This hub is being built to help you explore Ecuador’s Amazon with trusted highlights and clear planning.

What makes the Amazon special

Ecuador’s Amazon offers an extraordinary window into biodiversity and nature-first travel. From guided rainforest walks to river adventures and lodge stays, this region is about slowing down, listening, learning, and experiencing a living ecosystem up close.

Signature highlights

  • Wildlife spotting with experienced local guides
  • River experiences: canoeing, boat rides, and jungle access routes
  • Eco-lodges and nature-forward stays
  • Night walks and soundscapes that feel otherworldly
