Guayacanes Bloom in Loja (2026)
Region: Andes
Experience: Nature
Window: Jan 12 to Jan 22, 2026

The guayacanes bloom in Loja is one of Ecuador's most striking natural spectacles, and for 2026 there are confirmed dates. This is a short event that typically lasts about 5 to 8 days, so timing matters.

Key planning details

  • Confirmed period: Between January 12 and January 22, 2026.
  • Peak bloom: Expect the forest to be fully yellow between January 16 and January 18.
  • Planning note: Conditions can shift the peak slightly, so aim for the middle of the window if you can.

Carnival in Ecuador (2026)
Region: Andes
Experience: Culture
Window: Feb 14 to Feb 17, 2026

Carnival is one of Ecuador's most energetic national holidays, blending tradition with modern celebration. For 2026, expect a long weekend with big movement across the country, busy towns, and a strong festival atmosphere.

Key planning details

  • Main celebration period: Saturday, February 14 to Tuesday, February 17, 2026.
  • Peak days: The strongest parades and street energy are usually Sunday and Monday.
  • Travel tip: Book early and plan for heavier road traffic during the holiday window.

La Casa del Venado (Deer Farm) near Cayambe
Region: Andes
Experience: Nature
Window: Daily 06:00 to 18:00

In the Cayambe area is a deer farm, La Casa del Venado. There are a number of deer, along with some alpacas, cows, ducks and other animals. So how did this come about? An injured fawn was given to the owner of the farm to see if he could save it. The deer recovered and the farm later acquired another deer. After ten years of working with the deer, they opened a center to the public.

Key planning details

  • Shamans believe deer transmit more positive energy than any other animal, and that all humans can benefit by spending time with them.
  • These deer are not afraid of humans. You are encouraged to feed and pet them, helping to recharge your own energy.
  • Good place for families.
  • Hours: Open from 06:00 to 18:00.
  • Admission: $4 for adults and $2.50 for children.
