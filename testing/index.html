Baños

Baños is Ecuador’s adventure town with a relaxed, walkable center and easy access to waterfalls, viewpoints, and outdoor activities. Come for the scenery and adrenaline, stay for the hot springs and the small-town energy.

Region: Andes Best for: Waterfalls, Adventure, Viewpoints Typical Time: 2 to 3 Nights

What Baños Is Best For

Baños is the perfect base for a high-energy Andes trip. You can pack your day with waterfall stops and activities, then slow it down at night with hot springs, cafés, and a cozy town vibe.

Waterfall Route

The classic loop of big falls, short walks, and photo stops. Great by bike, taxi, or private driver.

Adventure Activities

Ziplining, canyoning, rafting, and swings with dramatic views. Options for most comfort levels.

Viewpoints and Sunsets

Quick hikes or rides to panoramic lookouts. Best at golden hour with clear skies.

Hot Springs Reset

A perfect recovery plan after a full day outside. Ideal for evenings and cooler nights.

Simple Planning Flow

Keep Baños simple: choose one big day for the waterfall route and activities, then leave time for viewpoints and a slower evening. Weather can shift quickly, so it helps to stay flexible.

Best Use Of Time

Baños is best when you treat it as a base, not a checklist. Do one strong adventure day, one scenic day, and keep one evening for hot springs and a calm dinner.
