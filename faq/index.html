FAQ

Experience Ecuador is built to make planning feel simple, clear, and inspiring. Here are the most common questions about how the site works and what is coming next.

How Experience Ecuador works

Q What is Experience Ecuador?

Experience Ecuador is a curated travel hub focused on Ecuador. We organize destinations by region and experiences by category so travelers can discover what is worth doing, compare options, and plan with confidence.

Q Are you a booking website?

Not right now. We are building an editorial guide first. For the time being, Experience Ecuador helps you explore and shortlist experiences and stays, then connect with trusted operators or partners.

Q How do you choose what gets featured?

We prioritize quality, safety, clarity, and traveler fit. Over time, featured listings will be based on a mix of on-the-ground research, partner information, guest feedback, and consistency of the experience.

Q What regions do you cover?

We are building coverage across Ecuador’s major travel regions: Galapagos, Coast, Andes, and the Amazon. Each region page will evolve into a complete guide with stays, planning tips, and curated experiences.

Q What kind of experiences will be included?

Experiences are organized by category so it is easy to browse: Adventure, Nature, Wildlife and Birding, Relaxation, Culinary, and Culture. Each category will include recommended spots, practical notes, and what it is best for.

Q Is the site content sponsored?

Some future partner placements may be paid, but the goal stays the same: clarity and trust. If a listing is sponsored or promoted, we intend to label it clearly so travelers can decide with confidence.

Q Do you sell or share my personal information?

No. If you contact us or join a newsletter in the future, your information is used only to communicate with you. You can review details anytime on the Privacy Policy page once it is published.

Q How can my lodge, tour, restaurant, or experience be featured?

We are actively building partnerships across Ecuador. Send a short introduction and what you offer to info@experienceecuador.com and include your location, website, and best way to contact you.

Q When will the guides and newsletter be available?

We are publishing region and experience pages first, then expanding them into structured guides. The newsletter will follow once there is enough curated content to deliver real value each issue.

Q Can you help plan a trip?

Yes, in a lightweight way. If you share your dates, interests, and budget range, we can point you to the right region pages and trusted partner options as the site grows. Reach out via Contact.

Quick notes
