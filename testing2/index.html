What partners get

Experience Ecuador is a planning layer. Travelers arrive with intent. They are deciding where to go, what to do, and who to trust.

High-intent visibility

  • Appears in Recommendations and can be filtered by region, location, experience, and category.
  • Built for discovery without noise. Visitors are planning, not doom-scrolling.
  • Direct action buttons to your website and WhatsApp.

Clean presentation

  • Square image carousel (auto-switch) with clear left and right controls.
  • Short write-up plus a "Why we recommend" snippet (optional).
  • Featured placement styling (available now for demo, paid later).

What we need from you

To publish a listing, we request the essentials below. You can submit in English or Spanish.

Assets

  • 3 to 4 square-friendly images (we can start with placeholders).
  • Business name and category (Lodging, Restaurants, Services, Activities).
  • Primary region and location(s). Multiple locations are supported.

Links and contact

  • Website URL (required for the primary button).
  • WhatsApp or phone (choose what you prefer for inquiries).
  • Short write-up (2 to 5 sentences). Optional: menu link or PDF.
