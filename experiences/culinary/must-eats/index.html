Must Eats

Ecuador’s regions are close on a map, but they taste completely different. The Andes brings hearty comfort food built for cool mountain mornings. The Coast is bold, citrusy, and seafood-forward. The Amazon leans into native ingredients and traditional preparation. The Galapagos keeps it simple with island flavors and fresh catch. Try one must-eat in each region and you will feel how geography, climate, and culture shape every bite.

Top must eats by region

Think of this page as your quick food compass. The same ingredients appear across Ecuador (plantain, corn, potatoes, fresh herbs), but each region transforms them in its own way. In the mountains you get warmth and richness. On the coast you get brightness and seafood depth. In the rainforest you get smoky, wrapped, and earth-forward cooking. On the islands you get simplicity and freshness. Start anywhere, then compare.
