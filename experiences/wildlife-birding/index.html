Wildlife & Birding

Ecuador is one of the most wildlife-rich countries on the planet. From hummingbirds and toucans to monkeys, frogs, and marine life, encounters are possible in every region. Use the selector below to match your style of wildlife travel.

What this experience is about

Wildlife and birding in Ecuador are not limited to remote expeditions. Short travel distances, expert local guides, and protected ecosystems make it one of the most accessible destinations in the world for biodiversity-focused travel. Whether you are a casual observer or a serious birder, Ecuador delivers exceptional encounters year round.
