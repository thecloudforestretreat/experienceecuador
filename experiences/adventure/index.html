Adventure in Ecuador

Ecuador is built for adventure. Volcanoes, rivers, jungles, coastlines, and cloud forests create endless opportunities for high energy experiences and unforgettable moments.

What defines adventure here

Adventure in Ecuador is compact, diverse, and accessible. In a single trip you can hike near active volcanoes, raft whitewater rivers, zipline through cloud forests, or explore remote terrain with expert local guides.

Popular adventure types

  • Hiking and volcano routes
  • Rafting and canyoning
  • Ziplining and canopy tours
  • Mountain biking and trail riding
  • Surfing and coastal adventures

Best regions for adventure

  • The Andes for altitude and volcano experiences
  • The Amazon for jungle and river exploration
  • The Coast for surf and ocean-based adventure
  • Galapagos for controlled, nature-driven exploration
