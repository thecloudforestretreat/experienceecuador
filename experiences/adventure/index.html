Adventure

Ecuador is built for adventure — volcanoes, rivers, coastlines, and cloud forests all within a few hours of each other. Use the selector below to match your energy level with the best-fit adventures and regions.

What this experience is about

Adventure in Ecuador is compact, diverse, and surprisingly accessible. You can combine high-altitude volcano routes, cloud forest canopy days, whitewater rivers, and coastal adventure into a single itinerary without losing days in transit. Whether you want something easy and scenic or full-throttle challenge, the country makes it simple to build an “active trip” that still feels balanced.

Pick your adventure level

Signature activities

  • Volcano hikes, ridgelines, and high-altitude viewpoints
  • Rafting, canyoning, and river adventure days
  • Ziplining and canopy tours in cloud forest terrain
  • Trail rides, mountain biking, and off-road excursions
  • Coastal surf days and ocean-forward adventures

Best for

  • Active travelers who like “do something every day” itineraries
  • Couples and small groups who want variety in a short trip
  • People who prefer local guides for safety + efficiency
  • Travelers who want to combine multiple regions without hassle

Regions that shine

  • Andes: volcano routes, altitude trails, adventure hubs
  • Amazon: river + jungle exploration with guide support
  • Coast: surf, ocean activities, beach adventure days
  • Galapagos: controlled exploration and iconic wildlife
