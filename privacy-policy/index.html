Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Experience Ecuador collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or contact us. We aim to keep this simple and transparent.

Effective date: January 1, 2025
Contact: info@experienceecuador.com

Overview

Experience Ecuador is a curated travel discovery website. We do not sell your personal information. We collect only what we need to operate the site and respond to messages.

Information we may collect

  • Contact information you provide: such as your name, email address, and message when you contact us.
  • Basic usage data: limited technical information that helps the site function, such as pages viewed and device or browser type.
  • Cookies: small files used for basic functionality and analytics. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and partnership requests.
  • To maintain and improve site performance, content, and navigation.
  • To protect the site against abuse, spam, or security risks.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies or similar technologies to help the website run smoothly and understand general traffic patterns. These tools may collect information such as browser type, approximate location (city or region level), pages visited, and time on site.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Some site features may not function as intended if cookies are blocked.

Note If we add a newsletter signup or other features later, this policy will be updated to reflect that change.

Sharing of information

We may share limited information only when necessary to operate the site and provide services. Examples include hosting providers, analytics tools, and email services used to communicate with you.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or to prevent fraud and abuse.

Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, such as responding to messages or maintaining site operations.

Your choices

  • You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you provided to us.
  • You can control cookies using your browser settings.
  • If we offer a newsletter in the future, you will be able to unsubscribe at any time.

To make a request, email info@experienceecuador.com.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Please avoid sending sensitive information through the contact email.

International visitors

If you access this site from outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be processed and stored across borders. By using the site, you consent to this processing where permitted by law.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the site evolves. When we do, we will revise the effective date above. Continued use of the site after updates means you accept the revised policy.

Quick summary

We collect minimal information to operate the site and respond to messages. We do not sell personal information.

  • Contact info only when you send it
  • Basic analytics for improvement
  • Cookie control via your browser
