About Experience Ecuador

Experience Ecuador is being built as a one stop hub for travel ideas, local highlights, and curated journeys across the country. From relaxation to adventure, culinary to cultural, the goal is simple: help travelers discover Ecuador with clarity and confidence.

What we are building

A thoughtfully curated platform that helps you explore Ecuador by region and by experience. We want to make it easy to discover what to do, where to stay, how to plan, and what is worth your time. Over time, this grows into a community and a digital newsletter where trusted local partners can showcase what they offer.

Built for travelers like you

  • International visitors looking for guidance and planning
  • Domestic travelers seeking weekend getaways
  • Eco tourists, birders, nomads, and adventure lovers
  • Retreat guests, food lovers, and culture seekers

What to expect next

  • Regional hubs: Galapagos, Coast, Andes, Amazon
  • Experience categories: relaxation, adventure, holistic, culinary, cultural
  • Newsletter highlights and featured partners
